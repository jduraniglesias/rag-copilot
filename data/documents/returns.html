Returns & Exchanges Policy

Effective date: Jan 1, 2024

Return Window

Return window: 30 days from delivery or pickup. Items must be in resalable condition with all accessories and original packaging.

To start a return, please include your order number and proof of purchase.

Refunds

After your item passes inspection, refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days. Processing times may vary by bank.

  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.
  • Gift cards are final sale and non-refundable.

Exchanges

Exchanges are allowed within 30 days, subject to stock availability and the condition of the returned item. If an exact replacement is unavailable, we can process a refund or offer store credit.

Restocking Fees

Non-defective items that are opened may incur up to a 15% restocking fee.

Non‑returnable Items

  • Final sale items
  • Digital downloads & software keys
  • Perishables and custom orders

How to Start a Return

  1. Contact support with your order_number and reason for return.
  2. We’ll issue an RMA (return merchandise authorization) if eligible.
  3. Ship the item using the provided label; include all accessories and packaging.
